About First Robotics Competition

First Robotics Competition embodies engineering as close as it can get. Teams of students are under strict rules with limited time and resources. They are tasked with raising funds, desiging a team brand, perfecting teamwork skills, alongside with building, and program large scale robots and compete against other like minded competitiors.
